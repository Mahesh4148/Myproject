History of Santubai Temple

Santubai Temple at Kadapur, in Chikodi taluk of Belagavi (Karnataka), is a revered local shrine believed by villagers to be around 200 years old, though this is mostly based on oral tradition. The temple is dedicated to the goddess Santubai, who is considered a protector and benefactor of children. Families from Kadapur and neighbouring villages often bring infants and young children here for blessings, reflecting the temple’s deep cultural and spiritual significance in community life. The shrine is beautifully situated beside a lake, which adds to its serene atmosphere and makes it a focal point for local gatherings and festivals. While detailed historical records are scarce, the temple’s location, traditional rituals, and continuous devotion highlight its importance as a living heritage site in northern Karnataka.
